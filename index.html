Variety: Do you listen to my music?



introduction


"Do you listen to my music" is bilibili launched the original music variety program,
by Xue Zhiqian, Dai Peni, Xu Song as the convener of spectrum village,
Qi Sijun as the host of the final, a total of 10. The program adopts the three-
dimensional scoring mechanism of "convener + Fresh listening group + tourist group".
The conveners of Youpu Village, fresh listening group and tourist group sit together and
discuss with the music lovers what is the good music of this generation of Chinese music.
